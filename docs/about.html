About U.N.D Industries

A multidisciplinary creative and technology organization built on music, vision, and continuous development.

The Organization

Universal Network
Development

U.N.D Industries is the umbrella for UND Tyzen's music, creative projects, and internal technology development. The name reflects the scope — universal in ambition, networked in structure, and always in development.

It is not limited to one medium or market. Music, visual storytelling, and technology work together as a single evolving ecosystem.

Everything built here is built to last, to scale, and to represent real creative ownership from the ground up.

🎵

Music First

Original releases and collaborations as the foundation of the brand. Available on all major platforms.

🔧

Internal Technology

Studio tools and systems built internally to support the creative workflow. Built for the work, not as public products.

🌐

Creative Universe

A visual and narrative universe expanding alongside the music. Character work, storytelling, and visual design in development.

The Artist

UND Tyzen

UND Tyzen is an independent artist building his sound, his vision, and his future from the ground up. He started with nothing but drive, faith, and a belief that growth comes from showing up — even when you're not ready.

What began as a quiet step into music turned into momentum. Winning a competition he barely planned for proved to him that purpose shows up when you do.

Now focused on consistency, elevation, and building on his own terms. Open for features, bookings, and select creative collaborations.
