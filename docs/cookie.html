Cookie Policy

Effective date: May 19, 2026

1. No Cookies Used

This site does not set, read, or use cookies of any kind. No cookie banner, consent dialog, or cookie preference manager is required because no cookies are placed on your device by this site.

2. No Tracking

There are no analytics tools, advertising networks, tracking pixels, or third-party scripts on this site that collect data about your visit. Your browsing behavior on this site is not recorded, analyzed, or shared.

3. localStorage Only

The only data stored on your device by this site is placed in your browser's localStorage — not in cookies. This data is limited to:

  • und_auth_user — a demo account object stored if you register (name, email, plaintext password).
  • und_auth_token — a randomly generated demo session token stored if you log in.

These values remain on your device only. They are not transmitted to any server and are part of the demo authentication system described in the Security Disclaimer.

4. Third-Party Embeds

This site embeds content from Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, and BandLab. These are independently operated services that may set their own cookies when you interact with their embedded players. U.N.D Industries has no control over those cookies. You can review their cookie practices through each platform's own privacy and cookie policies.

5. Changes

If cookies are ever introduced in a future version of this site, this Cookie Policy will be updated accordingly and a consent mechanism will be added where required by law. The most current version will always be available at this page (/cookie.html).
