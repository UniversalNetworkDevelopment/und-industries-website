Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 19, 2026

1. About This Site

U.N.D Industries operates this website as a static, front-end-only website. There is no backend server, no database, and no server-side processing of any kind. All functionality — including any account or login features — runs entirely within your web browser.

U.N.D Industries does not operate any server that receives, processes, or retains data submitted on this site.

2. Information We Collect

We do not automatically collect any personal information. The only data associated with your use of this site is data you voluntarily enter into forms or input fields, which may include:

  • A display name and email address if you register for an account on this site.
  • Messages or content you post in the member discussion area, if that feature is used.
  • Information voluntarily submitted through the contact form.

The contact form on this site does not transmit data to any server. A submission acknowledgement is displayed in the browser only. No data from the contact form is stored or forwarded to any destination.

3. How and Where Data Is Stored

Any data you enter on this site is stored locally in your browser using localStorage — a standard browser feature that keeps data on your own device. This data:

  • Does not leave your device.
  • Is not transmitted to U.N.D Industries or any third party.
  • Is not backed up, synchronized, or recoverable if deleted or lost.
  • Is accessible only within the browser on the specific device where it was created.

4. What We Do Not Collect

U.N.D Industries does not collect, use, or integrate any of the following:

  • Behavioral analytics or usage tracking of any kind.
  • Third-party advertising networks or ad-targeting technologies.
  • Third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or similar).
  • Cookies used for tracking, advertising, or persistent session management.
  • Device fingerprinting or browser profiling.
  • IP address logging.

5. Sensitive Data Warning

This site is not a secure data storage platform. You must not enter sensitive personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Passwords used on other services or platforms.
  • Financial information (credit card numbers, bank account details, etc.).
  • Government-issued identification numbers (Social Security, passport, driver's licence, etc.).
  • Medical or health information.
  • Any information you consider private or confidential.

U.N.D Industries is not responsible for any consequences arising from a user entering sensitive data on this site contrary to this warning.

6. Your Control & Data Deletion

Because all data is stored in your browser's localStorage, you have full and immediate control over it. You can delete all stored data at any time by:

  • Using any "Clear my data" or equivalent feature provided within the site's dashboard, if available.
  • Clearing your browser's site data or localStorage directly through your browser's settings (typically found under Settings → Privacy → Site Data or similar).

Clearing localStorage will permanently remove all locally stored information, including any account credentials, preferences, and discussion content associated with this site.

7. Data Sharing

U.N.D Industries does not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your data with any third party. No data you enter on this site is transmitted to any external service operated by U.N.D Industries or its affiliates.

8. External Links

This site contains links to third-party platforms including Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, BandLab, Instagram, and TikTok. These are independently operated services with their own privacy policies and data practices.

U.N.D Industries has no control over, and accepts no responsibility for, the privacy practices or content of any third-party service. Clicking a link to a third-party platform means leaving this site and entering a service governed by that platform's own terms and privacy policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

U.N.D Industries may update this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. The most current version will always be available at this page (/privacy.html). Continued use of the site after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
