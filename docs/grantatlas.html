What GrantAtlas Does

Built to get you funded.

Grant Discovery

Search a curated database of federal, state, local, and private grants. New programs are added continuously so you don't have to hunt across dozens of sites.

Eligibility Matching

Answer a one-time profile — your location, income range, industry, and goals. GrantAtlas filters out programs you don't qualify for so every result is worth your time.

Application Tracking

Save grants, track your status (researching, in progress, submitted, awarded), and get deadline reminders — all in one place so nothing slips through the cracks.

Secure & Private

Your profile data stays yours. GrantAtlas is a gated PWA — your information is never sold or shared with grant programs without your action.
